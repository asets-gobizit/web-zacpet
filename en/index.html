Our story

It started with one pet and a very clear idea.

ZacPet was born in Valencia — by people who believe pets aren't accessories, they're family. After years searching for products that combined quality, design and a bit of personality… we decided to make our own.

Behind the name, there's a personal story. Behind every product, hours of testing, tweaking, and a lot of approving tail wags.

Small brand, big heart. We start with a curated collection — and from there, we grow with you.
